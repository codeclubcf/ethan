Music Games

Featured Game
Up Beat

The best online music game EVER! Keep the rhythm to chart topping tracks and rock the party! Use the A, S, D, J, K, L, and Space Bar keys on your keyboard to play the notes that appear on the screen.

Amusix Violin

In Amusix: Violin you can be a master fiddler! Play classical, folk, and modern songs on your violin in this fluid, easy-to-understand game. Unlock the inner musician in you!

Dance Star Party Time

Trace the the controller over the action to make the characters move. Complete combo's of moves to get them dancing. Copy the action perfectly for maximum points.

Amusix Flute

To start the music press and hold the correct keys. Try to get the gold star by not making mistakes. You have to practice to get the songs just right.

Guitar Crazy

Are you ready to rock? As the song plays, watch the colored discs move down the screen. When the disc hits the bottom of the screen, hit the correct number on the keyboard.

Rad's Rockstar Hero

Get ready to become a rockstar hero. First choose your difficulty level then pick your song.

Rock-it Racer

Oh no! The "Just Call Me Harvey" rock band was on their way to a big concert in Franktown when they lost their music. Can you help?

Clique Girlz Dance Party

Arrows will fall to the beat of a Clique Girlz song. When they hit the diamonds press the arrow key that matches the falling arrow.

Franktown Rocks

Franktown Rocks is a fun mutliplayer online game for kids 8-12. You can create a character and walk around the city of Franktown and play with other characters.

Angela's Sound Stage

Welcome to Angela Anaconda's new sound effects suite. Her latest cartoon is nearly finished, but there is no sound yet. You need to go to the mixing machine and choose the sounds you think work best.

Drum Beats

Repeat the pattern of drum beats played by the computer. Press the left arrow key to hit the left side, and the right arrow key to hit the right side.

Musical Notes

Click the correct key as the musical notes appear.

Music Memory

Listen to the notes and then replay them by pressing the correct buttons.
